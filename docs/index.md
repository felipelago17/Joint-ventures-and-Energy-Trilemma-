# Joint Ventures and the Energy Trilemma

**PhD Research Repository** — Comparative governance analysis of upstream joint ventures in Norway and the UAE under competing energy-trilemma imperatives.

---

## Research Overview

This repository documents ongoing PhD research at the intersection of:

- **International energy law** — joint-venture governance, concession structures, JOAs
- **Energy policy** — the WEC energy trilemma (security, equity, sustainability)
- **Digital governance** — computational sovereignty in digital-oilfield partnerships

### Country Focus

| Dimension | Norway | UAE |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| Governance model | NPD licensing + Equinor SDFI | ADNOC concession equity |
| Regulator | NPD / PSA | Supreme Petroleum Council |
| Trilemma emphasis | Sustainability + Security | Security + Equity |
| Digital dimension | NPD data regulations | ADNOC AI strategy |
