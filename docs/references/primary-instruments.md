# Primary Legislative Instruments

BibTeX source file: [`references/primary-instruments.bib`](../references/primary-instruments.bib)

This file contains `@misc` and `@legislation` style entries for the primary statutory
instruments referenced across the Norway and UAE regulations registers.

**Norwegian instruments** (prefix `no_`): Continental Shelf Act 1963, Petroleum Act 1996,
Pollution Control Act 1981, CO₂ Storage Regulations 2014, CO₂ Safety Regulations 2020,
Petroleum Regulations Ch 4a, Exploitation Licence EL001 "Aurora".

**EU instrument** (prefix `eu_`): CCS Directive 2009/31/EC.

**UAE instruments** (prefix `ae_`): Constitution Article 23, Climate Law 2024,
Cabinet Decision No. 67 of 2024.
