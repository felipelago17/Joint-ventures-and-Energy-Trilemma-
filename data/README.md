# Data

Research data files for the JV & Energy Trilemma project.

## Files

### `ghasha-participating-interests.csv`

Indicative participating interests in the Hail and Ghasha ultra-sour gas concession
(Abu Dhabi). Columns: `participant`, `interest_pct`, `role`, `note`.

**Important:** these figures are indicative and rotate over the concession's life.
Confirm against primary concession filings and official ADNOC announcements before
any transactional, legal, or formally citable use.

Sources: [15], [16], [17], [19] in
[`references/uae-ccus-bibliography.md`](../references/uae-ccus-bibliography.md).

### `subscribers.txt`

Email addresses subscribed to the daily research digest.
Managed automatically by the GitHub Actions subscription workflow.
One address per line.
