# Regulations — UAE

UAE federal and Abu Dhabi emirate-level petroleum laws, ADNOC concession terms, and energy policy documents.

## Primary Legislation

| Document | Year | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| UAE Federal Law No. 4 | 1971 | Foundational petroleum law |
| Abu Dhabi Law No. 1 | 1974 | ADNOC establishment |
| UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 30 | 2023 | Competition (relevant to JV approvals) |
| UAE Cybercrime Law No. 34 | 2021 | Relevant to data governance in JVs |
| UAE Personal Data Protection Law | 2021 | PDPL — data in digital-oilfield JVs |

## Concession Framework

- ADNOC operates through operating companies (ADNOC Offshore, ADNOC Onshore, ADNOC Gas)
- IOC participation typically via equity stakes in these entities
- In-Country Value (ICV) programme requirements

## CCS/CCUS Sources — Al Reyadah JV

Primary sources on Al Reyadah (ADNOC 51% / Masdar 49%, later wholly acquired by ADNOC), the world's first commercial-scale CCS project in the iron and steel industry and MENA's first commercial CCUS project, commissioned November 2016. CO₂ captured from Emirates Steel Industries (ESI), Mussafah → 45 km pipeline → ADNOC onshore EOR fields.
